Will has over eight years experience in providing informatics,analytical and methodological solutions towards biomedical science. He currently holds a Professorship in Bioinformatics, Tianjin University, one of the top 25 universities in China, and is now pursuing advanced training in Harvard University. He has advised and collaborated on a wide variety of projects, including international work on biological network analysis, proteomics and genomics problems. Will has a head for unconventional thinking and has used it, on several occasions for solving fundamental analytical roadblocks in modern biology.
